# SFX Cues

## Movement

- `move_step_sand`: soft sandy footstep, short, dry.
- `move_bump_coral`: warm coral-stone bump, dull wooden/stone knock.
- `turn_swish`: tiny cloth and sea-breeze swish for turning.
- `stairs_descend`: watery downward slide with shell click.

## Combat

- `weapon_water_pistol_fire`: toy-like water pistol pop and splash.
- `weapon_water_hit`: wet splash impact, bright and harmless.
- `enemy_melee_whoosh`: quick anime slash/strike wind.
- `enemy_ranged_spray`: bubbly water spray burst.
- `player_damage`: small comedic impact, no gore.
- `enemy_damage`: short hit pop with splashy edge.
- `enemy_death_dissolve`: sparkling sand/heart particle dissolve.

## Items And Progression

- `pickup_potion`: coconut pickup pop, tiny bubble sparkle.
- `pickup_shells`: shell clatter and bright coin-like chime.
- `use_potion`: bubbly heal rise.
- `level_up`: short heroic sparkle fanfare.
- `level_start`: brief reveal flourish.
- `level_complete`: reward jingle.

## UI

- `ui_button`: soft shell click.
- `ui_modal_open`: breezy whoosh with chime.
- `ui_modal_close`: gentle shell tap.
- `debug_open`: tiny digital blip, understated.
- `debug_close`: lower blip.

## Death And Low Health

- `low_health_heartbeat_hearts`: soft cute pulse with heart sparkle.
- `defeat_heart_fill`: many small heart pops filling screen.
- `defeat_restart`: bright reset shimmer.
