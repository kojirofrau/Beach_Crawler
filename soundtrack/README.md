# Beach Crawler Audio Pack

Production notes for generating or recording Beach Crawler audio.

The target style is upbeat Asian RPG music from the late 1980s and early 1990s with a playful beach-adventure mood. Think bright FM synth bells, slap bass, marimba, shamisen-like plucks, small taiko/tom accents, hand claps, tropical percussion, and short melodic loops that do not get tiring.

## Contents

- `music_cues.md`: level soundtrack briefs and system music cues.
- `sfx_cues.md`: short sound-effect cue list for gameplay events.
- `voice_direction.md`: global voice acting rules and delivery format.
- `hero_lines.md`: hero voice profile and line variations.
- `enemy_swordswoman_lines.md`: aggressive swordswoman voice lines.
- `enemy_fighter_lines.md`: shy fighter voice lines.
- `enemy_gunslinger_lines.md`: cheerful shooter/gunslinger voice lines.
- `file_naming.md`: suggested output names and folder structure for generated audio.

## General Export Targets

- Music loops: `ogg` or `wav`, 44.1 kHz, seamless loop points.
- SFX: `wav`, short tail, normalized but not clipped.
- Voice: dry `wav`, no baked reverb, one line per file.
- In-game implementation can add compression, ducking, and reverb later.
