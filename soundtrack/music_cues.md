# Music Cues

## Global Style

Upbeat late-80s/early-90s Asian RPG soundtrack with beach flavor. Use bright melodic synths, FM bells, koto/shamisen-like plucks, tropical mallets, slap bass, light taiko/tom hits, brushed cymbals, and playful pentatonic hooks. Keep loops short, clean, and easy to layer.

Recommended loop length: 45-75 seconds for level themes, 8-20 seconds for stingers.

## Level Themes

### Floor 1 - Sunken Boardwalk

- Mood: first adventure, breezy, sunny, confident.
- Tempo: 118 BPM.
- Key: D major with pentatonic flourishes.
- Instruments: FM marimba lead, soft koto pluck, slap bass, light beach hand percussion, small tom fills.
- Loop: 64 bars.
- Generator prompt: "Create a seamless upbeat 16-bit Asian RPG beach dungeon loop, bright D major pentatonic melody, FM marimba lead, koto-like plucks, slap bass, gentle tropical percussion, playful but adventurous, late 80s/early 90s console style."

- Mood: colorful, busier, sparkling reef corridors.
- Tempo: 124 BPM.
- Key: G major.
- Instruments: bell synth arpeggios, bamboo flute lead, muted guitar/pluck ostinato, snappy snare, congas.
- Loop: 64 bars.
- Generator prompt: "Seamless retro Asian RPG coral reef dungeon theme, energetic G major, sparkling bell arps, bamboo flute lead, plucked ostinato, congas, snappy 16-bit drums, cheerful beach adventure."

### Floor 3 - Tide-Pool Ruins

- Mood: mysterious but still playful, tide pools and old stone.
- Tempo: 108 BPM.
- Key: A minor resolving often to C major.
- Instruments: low FM pad, shakuhachi-style breathy lead, kalimba, soft taiko, water droplet percussion.
- Loop: 56 bars.
- Generator prompt: "Seamless 16-bit Asian RPG beach ruins loop, playful mystery, A minor to C major, shakuhachi-like melody, kalimba, water droplets, soft taiko, warm ocean ambience, not dark."

### Floor 4 - Shellgate Reef

- Mood: heroic, sparkling, mid-run momentum.
- Tempo: 128 BPM.
- Key: E major.
- Instruments: shamisen-like lead, FM brass stabs, slap bass, upbeat tom groove, shell chime accents.
- Loop: 64 bars.
- Generator prompt: "Heroic upbeat retro Asian RPG reef maze theme, E major, shamisen-style lead, FM brass hits, slap bass, shell chimes, lively tom groove, sunny beach fantasy."

### Deeper Floor Variation

- Use the current floor theme family, but add one extra counter-melody and slightly denser percussion every four floors.
- Keep the same tempo and key center as that theme.

## System Music And Stingers

### Low Health

- Mood: cute panic, not horror.
- Length: 6-8 second loop.
- Instruments: pulsing bass, tiny heart-like blips, quick taiko taps.
- Prompt: "Short looping low-health warning cue, retro 16-bit Asian RPG, cute panic, pulsing bass, tiny heart blips, quick percussion, beach arcade tone."

### Level Start

- Mood: bright reveal.
- Length: 3-4 seconds.
- Prompt: "Short level start fanfare, retro Asian RPG beach adventure, bright bell melody, rising koto flourish, cheerful and clean."

### Level Complete

- Mood: rewarding and playful.
- Length: 5-7 seconds.
- Prompt: "Level complete jingle, 16-bit Asian RPG beach style, joyful pentatonic melody, shell chimes, upbeat tom flourish."

### Floor Transition / Descend

- Mood: sliding down to a new maze.
- Length: 2-3 seconds.
- Prompt: "Short descend transition sound, retro RPG, watery downward glissando, bamboo pluck, soft splash, cheerful."

### Defeat / Death

- Mood: comic defeat, heart-eyed, playful.
- Length: 6-8 seconds.
- Prompt: "Playful defeat jingle, retro Asian RPG, comedic heart-eyed KO feeling, descending melody then cute major chord, beach chimes, no horror."

### Potion Use

- Mood: refreshing, bubbly, instant relief.
- Length: 1-2 seconds.
- Prompt: "Short coconut potion healing sound, sparkling bubbles, beach chimes, retro 16-bit RPG, refreshing upward arpeggio."

### Enemy Defeated

- Mood: quick victory pop.
- Length: 1-2 seconds.
- Prompt: "Tiny enemy defeated sparkle burst, retro RPG beach style, bright chime, soft particle dissolve, upbeat."
