# Voice Direction

## General Recording Style

- Language: English.
- Tone: playful anime RPG, bright and slightly exaggerated.
- Performance: short barks, energetic, clean consonants.
- Avoid: grim horror, realism, heavy profanity, long speeches.
- Processing target: dry voice, no baked music, light compression only.

## Delivery Tags

Use these tags in generation prompts or recording notes:

- `[bright]`: smiling delivery.
- `[effort]`: short exertion, attack or movement.
- `[pain-light]`: quick comedic hurt.
- `[pain-heavy]`: defeat-level hurt, still non-gory.
- `[teasing]`: playful taunt.
- `[shy]`: quieter, uncertain.
- `[aggressive]`: sharp, confident.
- `[cheerful]`: upbeat and friendly.

## File Format

One line per audio file. Keep silence under 150 ms at head and under 300 ms at tail. Normalize consistently per character.
