```text
audio/
  music/
    floor_01_sunken_boardwalk_loop.ogg
    floor_02_coral_gallery_loop.ogg
    floor_03_tide_pool_ruins_loop.ogg
    floor_04_shellgate_reef_loop.ogg
    low_health_loop.ogg
    level_start_jingle.ogg
    level_complete_jingle.ogg
    floor_transition_descend.ogg
    defeat_jingle.ogg
  sfx/
    move_step_sand_01.wav

  voice/
    hero/
```

## Voice Naming

Use lowercase character, category, and two-digit variation number.

```text
hero_walk_01.wav
hero_damage_03.wav
hero_defeat_02.wav
swordswoman_taunt_01.wav
fighter_damage_02.wav
gunslinger_encounter_03.wav
```

## Music Naming

Use floor number, theme name, and loop/stinger type.

```text
floor_01_sunken_boardwalk_loop.ogg
floor_02_coral_gallery_loop.ogg
level_complete_stinger.ogg
defeat_heart_ko_stinger.ogg
```
